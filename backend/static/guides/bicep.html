How to Do a Bicep Curl(Left/Right):

Starting Position:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent.
  • Hold a dumbbell in your left/right hand with your palm facing forward.
  • Keep your arm fully extended, resting by your side.
  • Engage your core and keep your back straight.

Curl the Dumbbell:

  • Slowly bend your corresponding elbow to lift the dumbbell toward your shoulder.
  • Keep your upper arm stationary — only move your forearm.
  • Squeeze your bicep at the top of the movement.

Maintain Form:

  • Keep your wrist straight; don't let it bend backward.
  • Avoid swinging your body — control the movement with your arm.
  • Keep your elbow close to your torso.

Lower the Dumbbell:

  • Slowly lower the dumbbell back to the starting position.
  • Inhale as you lower the weight to maintain control.
  • Repeat for the desired number of reps, then switch sides if needed.
