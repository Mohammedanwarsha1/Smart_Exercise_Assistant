How to Do a Push-Up:

Starting Position:

  • Get into a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulders.
  • Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • Keep your arms straight, but don’t lock your elbows.

Lower Your Body:

  • Bend your elbows and lower your chest toward the floor.
  • Keep elbows at a slight angle, not flared out.
  • Lower yourself until your chest is close to or lightly touches the floor.

Maintain Form:

  • Keep your neck neutral — look slightly ahead, not straight down.
  • Don’t let your hips sag or rise too high; maintain the straight body line.

Push Back Up:

  • Press through your palms to straighten your arms and lift your body back to the starting position.
  • Exhale as you push up.
