How to Do a Squat:

Starting Position:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart.
  • Point your toes slightly outward.
  • Keep your chest up and your back straight.

Engage Your Core:

  • Tighten your abdominal muscles to support your lower back.

Lower Your Body:

  • Push your hips back like you’re sitting in a chair.
  • Bend your knees and lower yourself down.
  • Keep your weight on your heels, not your toes.

Go as Low as Comfortable:

  • Ideally, lower until your thighs are parallel to the floor.
  • Make sure your knees don’t go past your toes.

Rise Back Up:

  • Push through your heels to stand back up.
  • Squeeze your glutes as you return to standing.
